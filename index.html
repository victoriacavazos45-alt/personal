About

Eight years as a Surface Warfare Officer. Four deployments — one to the Black Sea aboard USS Ross out of Rota, Spain, and three in the Caribbean and South America on USS Wichita and USS St. Louis. I've walked the streets of Valparaíso, stood watch in the Eastern Mediterranean, and coordinated with Chilean and Peruvian navies in Spanish during UNITAS 2025.

I have a B.A. in Spanish from St. Edward's University in Austin. I've written for Proceedings, Business Insider, and the Brownsville Herald. I run a 5-star Airbnb in Jacksonville Beach. I'm currently the Mission Liaison Officer at Littoral Combat Ship Squadron TWO in Mayport, and I'm transitioning out in June 2026.

I'm looking for what's next — in planning, business operations, or tech/media. I bring operational rigor, a global perspective, and the ability to write a sentence that people actually want to read.
